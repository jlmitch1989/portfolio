About Me

I'm a Coast Guard Veteran and a recent recipient of a bachelor's degree in Holistic Psychology from Lesley University. Serving in the U.S. Coast Guard requires a sense of honor, respect, and devotion to duty. I have learned to implement these core values in everything I set out to do. Tie that with a degree in holistic psychology, and you have someone who works extremely hard, and loves working with people. My past success has structured my foundation, and has helped me to begin building my path towards a successful career in web development. It is in web development, that I have found true job satisfaction, and an outlet in which to cultivate my creativity and ingenuity. As an individual and most importantly, as a team member, I bring a lot to the table. With my eagerness, and motivation to learn I would make for a great asset to any team.
